ARTIST STATEMENT

It’s all about freedom and abandon!  My garments are an ode to the short-lived  freedom of the 1970’s disco era.  After the Vietnam War, Watergate, and the ongoing  Civil Rights conflict that American’s were enduring, people just wanted to dance, wear sparkly clothes and make-up and let their inner selves be free.  Each night,  clubs would open their doors, and patrons of all types could dance the night away.   My clothes were inspired by the fashions of the disco era and the desire to have that freedom of abandon again during these pandemic times.  

ARTIST BIO

Christine Beaman returned to Minneapolis after being away for 25 years to pursue her AAS degree in Apparel Technologies at Minneapolis College. She will be moving to Atlanta, Georgia, to work in the film industry, Local 479.

Models: Brittany Baertsch, Caira Iseminger, Mary Stephens, Jamaal Jarmon
