ARTIST STATEMENT

My designs are reflections of my emotions and a way to express what I feel in the moment. Getting to know the person I am designing for is very important to me. Learning more about the person – my client – inspires me to create the best look for them. I am always looking for new sources for inspiration and experimenting with shapes, colors, and fabrics.

ARTIST BIO

Fashion designer Paulet Nava was born in Mexico. She remembers designing and creating outfits from scratch ever since she was a young girl. Paulet studied fashion design in Mexico. She attended Per-Lei School for Design to pursue a career in fashion. While studying, she had the opportunity to participate in the “B – FIV” (fashion international view) contest, where her illustration was voted one of the best in Latin America. She graduated in 2013, obtaining the best generation grades. To gain hands-on experience to become a seasoned fashion designer, Paulet opened her own business and produced her clothing brand “Pupila.” In 2015, she moved to the United States to learn a new language and immerse herself in a new culture. After two years in the United States, she decided to study Apparel Technologies at Minneapolis College to get additional experience and have more opportunities to develop in different areas of design. While studying at Minneapolis College, she had the opportunity to participate in the American Heart Association’s “Go Red for Women” fashion show and competition at the Mall of America, where she won 1st place.
